About Balaji Farms

Where Nature Meets
Luxury & Comfort

Balaji Farm is not just a getaway — it is an experience. Nestled in the lush countryside near Mumbai, our property offers the perfect blend of rustic charm and modern luxury. Whether you are planning a dream wedding, a corporate retreat, or a peaceful family weekend, we provide an exclusive sanctuary away from the city's noise.

With two beautifully designed 4 BHK properties, expansive lawns, and world-class hospitality, every moment spent here becomes a cherished memory. Our team ensures personalized attention, farm-fresh cuisine, and seamless event execution from start to finish.
